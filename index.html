The Digital Intelligence Mission

As a San Francisco Bay Area nonprofit, Digital Intelligence is dedicated to bridging the regional digital divide. We partner with local community centers and libraries to ensure tech access for all residents.

We believe that technology is a key to opportunity from finding a job and managing personal finances to connecting with loved ones.

"Our mandate is to ensure every Bay Area resident, regardless of background or prior experience, has the confidence and skill set to comfortably navigate today's digital world."
